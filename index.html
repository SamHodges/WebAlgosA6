Animated Convex Hull Demo

Click on the box below to add points. After you have a couple of points on the screen, you can start playing with the buttons below.

Clicking on Start primes the site to iterate through the points by sorting them by x-coordinate, using height as a tie breaker should any two x-coordinates be equal.
Clicking on Step checks the highlighted point as the first of a three-point turn- each of the steps in the following sequence would count as ONE step:
1. if said turn moves right, the point will be connected by a line to the following point.
2. Wherever there is no right turn, the algorithm backtracks (1 Step at a time) until there is another path to take.
The button Full Animation iterates through all the steps in one go.
Click Stop Animation to stop the next step in the hull animation.
